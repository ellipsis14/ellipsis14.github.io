Yog (self + divine)

All life is yog. I first became a yogi and then started pursuing science.

Philosophy

To my personal conviction, everyone is striving for peace. A billionaire wants to have peace with money, Scientist is seeking for peace with his research. Our mind is indulged in constant chatter (24*7). Materialistic accomplishment is not a luxury these days. Focused mind and concentration is a luxury. In this era of consumerism, We are bombarded with distractions on all fronts and we seek social validations. If we live aside our sleeping schedule, we provide multiple subjects to our mind every second.

The moment we get up, our mind displays a long list of indulgences. What is right, and what is wrong are the confines of mental arena.

What is needed can be only be pursued if we understand mind, its origin and subdue it at the location from where it originates. This is where a yogi starts his journey.

Info

Yog has two levels - spiritual (the zone of consciousness) and physical (the zone of Prakriti, that is, the material world). Mantra Yoga, Laya Yoga, Raja Yoga, Hatha Yoga - all these are in the zone of Prakriti, which means, all these types of yoga are Prakritik, that is, of the material world. They are all based on the Kundalini, Prana and Mind. They are performed mainly in the zone extending from the Muladhar chakra upto the Brahmrandhra, that is, the Tenth Door in the human body. All their practices are confined to the Pind (the human body), and the Brahmand (the cosmos); and they are performed in the zone of Prakriti using Prakritik prana, mind and the breath.
